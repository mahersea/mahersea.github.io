Mission Overview

The Mass Effect series takes place in the late 22nd century, where humanity has discovered mass relays - ancient devices that enable faster-than-light travel between star systems.

The Setting

These relays, left behind by the Protheans, connect humanity to a vast galactic community organized under the Citadel Council. The galaxy teems with diverse species, each with their own cultures, technologies, and political agendas.

Your Mission

As Commander Shepard, you become the first human Spectre - an elite agent tasked with maintaining galactic peace and uncovering ancient threats that could destroy all organic life.

Galactic Species Database

Asari

Long-lived, biotic-capable species known for diplomacy and wisdom.

Turians

Militaristic hierarchy-based society with strong sense of duty.

Salarians

Highly intelligent amphibians excelling in science and espionage.

Krogan

Warrior race recovering from the genophage, a biological weapon.

Quarians

Nomadic species living in environmental suits aboard their fleet.

Geth

Synthetic intelligences created by the Quarians, now independent.

Mission Timeline

Mass Effect (2007)

Shepard uncovers Saren's alliance with the Reapers and prevents their immediate return to the galaxy.

Mass Effect 2 (2010)

After being resurrected by Cerberus, Shepard builds a team to stop the Collectors' human harvesting operation.

Mass Effect 3 (2012)

The Reapers invade. Shepard must unite the galaxy's fractured civilizations for a final stand at Earth.

Technology Database

Mass Relays

Ancient Prothean constructs that enable faster-than-light travel by manipulating mass effect fields.

Biotics

Individuals exposed to element zero can manipulate mass effect fields, granting telekinetic abilities.

Omni-Tools

Multi-purpose devices that serve as computers, fabricators, and hacking tools all in one.

Element Zero (Eezo)

Rare material that, when subjected to electrical current, creates mass effect fields that alter the mass of objects.
